Tech Demos

  • MPEG-DASH demo with Video.js


    Use MPEG-DASH, an adaptive-bitrate streaming technology to make your streaming faster than ever in this demo with Video.js (without Video.js is also an option). Everybody uses it, including YouTube, so learn how to do it yourself!

  • Ruffle demo with sample SWF


    Demo of Ruffle, a Flash emulator that works with ActionScript 1, 2, and 3, written in Rust to mitigate Flash's security issues that is usable both natively and on the web. It's very powerful and doesn't use up too much resources, especially in native versions.
